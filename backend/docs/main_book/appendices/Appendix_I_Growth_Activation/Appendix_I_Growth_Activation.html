APPENDIX I – GROWTH & ACTIVATION ENGINE

FinderOS Main Book – Mandatory Appendix

I.1 Strategic Purpose

Finder_OS Growth & Activation Engine exists solely to produce product-specific, geo-precise, high-intent leads.

I.2 Supply-First Growth Model

Growth starts with verified supply. Demand is never simulated.

I.3 Geo-Precision Targeting

Targeting radius: minimum 20m, maximum 50m, default 30m.

I.4 Zero-Point Activation

Activation occurs at the point of need, not search intent.

I.5 Product-Centric Lead Definition

All leads are category and product specific.

I.6 Finder_OS ↔ FINDIAG Boundary

Finder_OS generates leads. FINDIAG executes matching and service flow.

I.7 Monetization Neutrality

Growth engine is monetization-neutral by design.

I.8 Data as Strategic Asset

Accumulated demand-supply intelligence is the core long-term asset.

I.9 Minimum Compliance Rules

Geo precision, category enforcement, and zero fake activation are mandatory.
