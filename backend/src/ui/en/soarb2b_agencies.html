The agency problem

Agencies often face:

  • Ad budget exists but the right target is unclear
  • “Leads” come in but they’re not decision-makers
  • Clients ask: “What did we get for this spend?”
  • Platforms lack transparency

SOAR B2B addresses this.

What SOAR B2B gives agencies

1. Real target list — Businesses by geography, address, contact, ownership (where available), pre-validated (official + online sources).

2. Persona engine — Role (Procurement, CEO, Ops…), seniority, department, channel fit (email / LinkedIn / web). Persona = reachability + intent, not just a label.

3. Precision exposure — Right location, right context; optionally with the agency’s own ad account or SOAR partners.

4. Soft conversion → direct outreach — Site visit, content engagement, optional meeting / contact.
