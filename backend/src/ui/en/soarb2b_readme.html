How SOAR B2B works: steps, outputs, and usage.

1. Create a meeting plan

From the homepage, use Build Your Meeting Plan to start the 5-question onboarding. Specify target region, product or service type, and business type. The system adds an HS (customs) code to the plan from your input or automatic inference.

2. Objective selection and timeline

After the plan is created, select target modules (e.g. market scan, decision-maker targeting). Track step status on the process timeline page. After approval, analysis and targeting run.

3. Results hub

The results hub shows verified company lists, contact channels (email, LinkedIn, etc.), and export options. You can export data to CRM or ad platforms.

4. Pricing

Free: 1 query per month, 1 company. Premium: Pay-as-you-go, per-query pricing. Agency: Multiple companies, API, dedicated support. See the Pricing page for current plans.

5. Support

Reach us via the Contact page form or email. A live support widget is also available.
