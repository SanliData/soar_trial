What is SOAR B2B?

SOAR B2B helps manufacturers and wholesalers reach verified businesses and convert attention into booked meetings. We focus on decision-first targeting: identifying the specific roles and departments that make purchasing decisions in your target companies.

You control the activation. We provide verified company lists, decision-maker mapping, and targeting data. You choose how and when to engage—through your own outreach, advertising platforms, or direct channels.

No automated messaging. No hidden advertising costs. No meeting guarantees. You own the relationship and the results.

How SOAR B2B Works

SOAR B2B provides verified company data and decision-maker targeting. Here's what you need to know:

No Automatic Outreach

We do not send messages, emails, or any form of communication on your behalf. You maintain full control over all outreach activities.

No Hidden Advertising Costs

Your subscription covers data access and targeting tools. Any advertising spend on platforms like LinkedIn, Meta, or Google Ads is separate and controlled entirely by you.

User-Controlled Activation

You decide how to use the data we provide. Export to your CRM, build advertising audiences, or use it for direct outreach. The activation method and timing are entirely your choice.

No Meeting Guarantees

We provide targeting data and tools. We do not guarantee booked meetings or specific results. Your success depends on how you activate the data, the quality of your messaging, and market conditions.

SOAR B2B is a decision-first targeting platform. We help you identify the right companies and roles. You build the relationships.

Legal & Compliance Notice

SOAR B2B is a decision-support platform. It does not provide legal advice and does not guarantee business outcomes, meetings, or responses.

Data & sources

SOAR B2B may reference publicly available information and user-provided inputs. Users are responsible for ensuring their outreach, advertising, and communications comply with applicable laws, platform policies, and industry regulations, including but not limited to privacy, marketing, and anti-spam requirements.

User control & consent

Any activation of paid channels, messaging, advertising, or direct contact is initiated only by the user. SOAR B2B does not impersonate users or send messages without explicit authorization.

Third-party services

If users connect external services (e.g., advertising platforms, analytics, CRMs), those services' terms and policies apply.

This is not legal advice.
