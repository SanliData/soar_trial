About

SOAR B2B is a B2B meeting engine that helps manufacturers and wholesalers reach verified businesses and convert attention into booked meetings.

Our mission

Reach the right companies, target the right decision-makers, and turn contacts into meetings. You control activation; we provide data and targeting tools.

What we offer

Verified company lists: Business locations with addresses, contacts, and websites; exportable to CRM.

Decision-maker targeting: Roles and departments that make purchasing decisions; compatible with LinkedIn, Meta, and Google Ads.

Location-accurate reach: Geo-targeting for radius-based campaigns; verified addresses.

Principles

No automatic outreach: We do not send messages or emails on your behalf.

No hidden ad costs: Your subscription covers data access and targeting tools; ad spend stays under your control.

No meeting guarantee: Results depend on how you use the data and market conditions.
