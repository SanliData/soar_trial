***REMOVED*** Operational motion rules (SOAR B2B)

Motion in FinderOS is an operational aid, not decoration.

***REMOVED******REMOVED*** Principles

- Motion supports cognition: clarify state changes, hierarchy, and cause/effect.
- Prefer **no motion** over ambiguous motion.
- Keep motion subtle, short, and consistent.

***REMOVED******REMOVED*** Allowed motion

- **Panel / drawer expansion**: `details` open/close and drawer transitions.
- **Timeline expand**: reveal details with a small height/opacity transition.
- **Focus transitions**: focus ring appearance for keyboard navigation.
- **Loading shimmer**: minimal, low-contrast, short-lived; avoid continuous shimmer.

***REMOVED******REMOVED*** Disallowed motion

- Decorative animations (hero motion, bouncing, pulsing badges).
- Oversized transitions that pull attention away from the work.
- Auto-scrolling feeds or forced motion that increases fatigue.
- “AI magic” effects (glow pulses, typing simulations).

***REMOVED******REMOVED*** Timing & easing (guidance)

- Duration: 120–180ms for small transitions; 180–240ms for drawers.
- Easing: ease-out for open; ease-in for close.
- Reduce motion: respect user/system preferences when available (`prefers-reduced-motion`).
